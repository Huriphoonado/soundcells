The current Soundcells prototype supports a subset of the ABC music syntax.

Note

Every note must contain a pitch and may optionally contain an accidental, octave, and duration.

  • Accidental: _ = ^
  • Pitch: abcdefgABCDEFG
  • Octave: , '
  • Duration: Number or Fraction
Chord

A chord is a group of notes surrounded by square brackets. The duration of the first note, sets the chord duration.

Example: [c4 e g]

Header

A header provides information about the music and/or abc notation. Each header is on its own line and consists of a capital letter, colon, and value.

  • Number. X: 1
  • Title. T: A Title
  • Composer. C: Name
  • Key Signature. K: C
  • Time Signature. M: 4/4
  • Note Duration. L: 1/4
  • Tempo. Q: 120
