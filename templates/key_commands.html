Most soundcells (SC) commands begin with shift + ctrl (cmd on mac.)

Nav
  • Jump to Editor or Braille Music SC + 9
  • Display Errors SC + m
  • Open Save Menu ctrl + s
Music Playback
  • Play/Pause SC + space
  • Stop SC + .
  • Toggle Loop Mode SC + l
Information (In Editor)
  • Current Measure ? then m
  • Current Note ? then n
